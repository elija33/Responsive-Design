Goals

Together Is Better Than One

Ghanaman Association of USA is a converged forum for Ghanaians community within New Jersey and its neighboring states. Ghanaman serves as an umbrella organization for Ghanaians living in New Jersey and its neighboring states with sole aim of acting as a mouthpiece for its members and Ghanaians community in New Jersey to enhance the living standards of Ghanaian-USA in New Jersey through social, cultural, and educational programs, and through other diaspora initiatives. Ghanaman Association of USA takes pride in preserving Ghana culture whiles maintaining our true ancient cultural values and beliefs that have being passed down by our ancestors which our board members and its active members use to shape their lives and are openly ready to teach and mentor the Ghanaians and its younger generation living in New Jersey. The Association operates with ethics, honesty and decency in everything we do so we can uphold integrity and gain respect from members, soon to join and non-members. We provide great support to our active members so they can be empowered, encouraged and be motivated to tackle all aspect of their life within their respective community. Our goal is to equip the Ghanaian generation of today so they can value and respect Ghana culture and pass it to the generation yet to come.
